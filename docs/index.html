AI-powered contraindication screening for private practice

Cross-reference medications, conditions, and allergies against any procedure, reliably and instantly. Practitioners shift from manual lookups to confident, highly informed decisions.

1

Contraindication: a vital process for private practice risk and quality, but how robust is it?

  • The BNF covers drug interactions, not procedure-specific contraindications
  • SmPCs describe side effects, not clinical protocols for aesthetic or dental contexts
  • NICE guidelines follow NHS pathways, not private clinic workflows
  • Practitioners synthesise across 5+ disconnected sources, per patient, per procedure, under time pressure
  • No single tool connects a patient's full profile to the specific risks of a planned procedure
2

...with an evolving landscape, expertise in demand and fragmented data sources, the impacts of errors are catastrophic

  • New medications, biologics, and GLP-1 agonists reaching market quarterly. Guidelines cannot keep pace
  • Clinical negligence claims rising year on year. A missed contraindication is indefensible in court
  • New UK licensing requirements demand documented, auditable pre-procedure safety checks
  • NHS hospitals embed automated alerts in every prescribing system. Private practice bears all the risk, with none of the tooling
3

...teams do not need replacing, they need to be converted from data processors to highly informed decision-makers, quickly

  • Cross-references medications, conditions, and allergies against any planned procedure in seconds
  • Draws on BNF, NICE guidelines, SmPCs, MHRA safety updates, and pharmacovigilance databases. Always current
  • Deterministic and auditable. Every finding is traceable to a named clinical source
  • Practitioner time freed for the clinical judgement calls only they can make

Built by former PwC AI and regulatory compliance specialists, alongside a medico-legal clinical assessor who sees the consequences when pre-procedure checks fail.
